Pop Corn

Description

Bored of vanilla pop corns? Try this spicy pop corn and va-la-la!

Ingredients

  • 100gr pop corn
  • Sunflower oil
  • Curry powder and thyme

Steps

  1. Heat the pan for like 1 minutes
  2. Pour the oil
  3. Put the pop corns
  4. Put some curry powder and thyme and mix it with wooden spoon
  5. Shake the pan few times until it is ready
  6. Enjoy
