Hummus

Description

Hummus is a very delicious dish which is quite popular in Turkey. It is especially tasty if you are drinking some liquor! :)

Ingredients

  • 500gr of boiled chickpeas
  • 100ml of olive oil
  • 100ml of tahini
  • 3-4 pieces of garlics
  • salt, two lemons juice, cummin and pepper powder

Steps

  1. put everything into the blender
  2. start crushing everything
  3. taste it on the way and modify it to your taste
  4. after it becomes creamy, take it out to a plate or your preferred box
  5. pour some extra olive oil and lemon juice
  6. bon appetite!
